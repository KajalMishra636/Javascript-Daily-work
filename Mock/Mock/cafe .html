About Brew & Bean

Founded in 2020, Brew & Bean has been serving the community with the finest coffee and pastries. We source our beans from sustainable farms and roast them to perfection in our own facility.

Our café is more than just a place to grab coffee – it's a community hub where friends meet, ideas are born, and memories are made.
